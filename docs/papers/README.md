## Relationship to Civic Inspection Lab

The paper is the published research foundation for the broader question of equitable,
capacity-constrained municipal complaint classification. Civic Inspection Lab is a portfolio
implementation that narrows to a reproducible, interactive NYC after-hours-variance case
study. It independently rebuilds its data pipeline, labels, simulator, validation protocol, and
public claims. It must not imply that its future metrics reproduce the paper's multi-domain
results unless a specific experiment has been rerun and documented here.

The earlier local draft remains restricted and is not distributed. The published arXiv version
is the sole citable paper artifact in this repository.
